Hunt for a heart

  • High Concept: A game of collecting the pieces of organs to give to a girl who needs a transplant.
  • Player Experience Goals: Competition as they try to get as many hearts as they can in 5 minutes.
  • Genre: Casual game.
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move and jump to collect the randomly spawned heart on the map and return each one to the girl at the start point.
  • Prototype goal: Randomly spawning objectives and returning them to a particular point.
  • Features: The randomly spawning objectives.
  • Setting/Premise: A girls needs a heart so it is up to you to collect the missing pieces of a heart and return them to her.
  • Story: NA
  • Target Audience: Casual players with a couple of minutes to burn.
  • Play time: 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Efficient search patterns will be a strong way to do well at this game.
